Scaling & Dynamic Fit

Choose the desired behavior for how the game fills the screen

A

Aspect Ratio Lock (Current)

Stays exactly 4:3. Shrinks to fit the smallest dimension. Reliable but can leave empty "letterboxing" on wide screens or tall phones.

B

Fluid Grid Expansion

The grid itself adds/removes cells to fill the entire screen. Larger screens see more of the ocean; smaller screens see a tighter sector. Feels "native" on all devices.

C

Smart Zoom / Stretch

Keeps the 20x15 grid but allows the cells to grow/shrink independently in width and height to fill the box, OR scales the whole view to fill the screen (cropping some edges).
